twoseater

sculpture, mdf and mirrors, 1996
prev  next

Two people can sit back to back in this sculpture. Their seats are the two ends of the same piece of wood. Thi means that they will be able to feel eachothers movements. They can see eachother through the tube, which has four mirrors and functions as a double periscope.
